# 30-Day Income Roadmap

Updated: 2026-06-27

## Week 1: Fast Validation

- Build premium offer.
- List 10 old clients.
- Draft 3 content posts.
- Track expense offset target.

## Week 2: Booking Focus

- Mark approves scripts.
- Manually contact selected old clients.
- Offer 2-3 available time slots.
- Track replies and booking rate.

## Week 3: Productize Knowledge

- Turn exam/study material into a small sample.
- Draft ROM/MMT or physical modality mini product.
- Write AI assistant case study.

## Week 4: Measure And Decide

- Count revenue.
- Count inquiries.
- Count bookings.
- Identify highest converting offer.
- Stop low-converting or high-cost experiments.

## Expected Revenue Ranges

- One premium session: 2,800-3,800 TWD.
- 4-session package: 9,800-13,800 TWD.
- 8-session package: 18,800-28,800 TWD.
- Study product: 199-999 TWD per sale.
- AI assistant consulting pilot: 5,000-30,000 TWD.

## Targets

- Firebase USD 25: offset with one small sale or one session.
- 11,840 warning spending: offset with one 4-session package or two premium sessions.
- Monthly cashflow gap: requires repeated client bookings or package sales.
