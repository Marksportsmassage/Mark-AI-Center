# Video Content Income Plan

Updated: 2026-06-27

## Content Funnel

1. Short educational video.
2. Viewer recognizes a problem.
3. Call to action: ask for a draft assessment / session slot.
4. Mark reviews and replies manually.
5. Session or package offer only after fit is clear.

## 3 Short Video Scripts

### Script 1: Shoulder stiffness that keeps returning

- Hook: "肩頸一直緊，不一定是你不夠放鬆。"
- Body: explain load, posture, recovery, and movement pattern.
- Demo: one safe shoulder blade movement.
- CTA: "如果你想知道是哪一段卡住，可以先做一次狀態評估。"
- Safety: no diagnosis, no guaranteed result.

### Script 2: Hip mobility for people who train

- Hook: "深蹲卡住，不一定只是髖太緊。"
- Body: explain hip, ankle, trunk, and training load.
- Demo: one mobility screen concept.
- CTA: "我可以幫你整理一份改善方向草稿。"

### Script 3: Why soreness comes back

- Hook: "按完舒服，但幾天後又回來，通常代表還有一件事沒做。"
- Body: explain recovery plus behavior / movement habit.
- Demo: one simple self-check.
- CTA: "想要不只是放鬆，可以做 4 次改善方案。"

## 3 Animation / Visual Explainer Scripts

### Animation 1: Pain loop

- Scene 1: stress / training load increases.
- Scene 2: tissue feels tight.
- Scene 3: massage gives relief.
- Scene 4: same load returns.
- Scene 5: plan adds movement and recovery.

### Animation 2: Package logic

- Scene 1: single session = assessment and immediate relief.
- Scene 2: 4 sessions = pattern correction.
- Scene 3: 8 sessions = habit and performance support.

### Animation 3: Expense offset

- Scene 1: warning spending appears.
- Scene 2: system creates offset target.
- Scene 3: one package or two sessions offset cost.
- Scene 4: no new spending until offset action exists.

## Publishing Rules

- Draft first.
- Mark reviews before posting.
- No medical diagnosis.
- No guaranteed recovery.
- No paid ads.
- No auto posting.
