# High Income Execution Plan

Updated: 2026-06-27

Goal: raise income without adding high-cost tools, ads, installments, or external automation.

Research inputs:

- Physical therapy and sports massage businesses commonly package outcomes into assessments, multi-session plans, and follow-up support.
- Local service reactivation is usually faster than cold audience building because trust already exists.
- Short video lead generation works best when it shows a problem, a simple explanation, a safe next step, and a clear call to action.
- AI automation consulting can be sold as small scoped workflow improvement, not as vague AI magic.

## Today: 30-Minute Tasks

1. Write the premium offer menu.
   - Single session: 2,800-3,800 TWD.
   - 4-session package: 9,800-13,800 TWD.
   - 8-session improvement plan: 18,800-28,800 TWD.
   - Output: one draft card, not a public post.

2. List 10 old clients.
   - Name / issue / last contact / likely need / safe message angle.
   - Output: review board only.

3. Pick 3 short video topics.
   - Neck/shoulder stiffness.
   - Hip mobility for training.
   - Why repeated soreness returns.
   - Output: scripts only.

## Today: 2-Hour Tasks

3. Record or outline 3 short videos.
4. Create one expense-offset tracker.

## 7-Day Sprint

1. Day 1: offer, old-client list, 3 content ideas.
2. Day 2: finish 4-session and 8-session package drafts.
3. Day 3: Mark reviews old-client scripts.
4. Day 4: publish or schedule one approved professional post.
5. Day 5: outline exam/study mini product.
6. Day 6: write AI assistant case study.
7. Day 7: measure inquiries, bookings, revenue, and offset progress.

## 30-Day Revenue Roadmap

Week 1:

- Validate service offer.
- Reactivate old clients.
- Publish 3 useful posts or scripts.

Week 2:

- Convert interested clients into sessions or packages.
- Build the ROM/MMT or physical modality study product outline.

Week 3:

- Create AI assistant case study page.
- Offer one small workflow consulting pilot.

Week 4:

- Review revenue, cost offset, and what converted.
- Kill low-converting ideas.
- Double down on the strongest offer.

## Expense Offset Targets

- Firebase USD 25: one small session, one study product sale, or one consulting micro-task can cover it.
- Line Pay warning spending 11,840 TWD: one 4-session package, two premium sessions, or four lower-priced sessions can offset it.
- Monthly cashflow gap: requires recurring service bookings or package sales, not one-time tiny products.

## Do Not Do Now

- Do not buy ads.
- Do not add installments.
- Do not pay for new software.
- Do not auto-message clients.
- Do not promise medical outcomes.
- Do not treat toy / lottery spending as an asset without cost table, resale value, recovery deadline, and stop loss.
